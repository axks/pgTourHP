Students from the PionierGarage, the students group for entrepreneurship at the Karlsruhe Institute for Technology in Germany, are going visit the worlds leading tech-region.

About us

The PionierGarage is going back to the USA! After three inspiring tours to the Sillicon Valley, the PionierGarage is this year going to Boston and New York. Watch the amazing 2013 Tour video! 2014, we are full of energy to come back with a new group of young entrepreneurs.
